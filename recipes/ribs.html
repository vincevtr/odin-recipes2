EZ Ribs

Description

Best fall off the bone ribs ever

Ingredients

  • Pork baby back ribs
  • BBQ Sauce
  • Paprika
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic powder

Steps

  1. Mix together paprika, salt, black pepper, onion powder, and garlic powder
  2. Dust Ribs with dry rub misture from previous step
  3. Bake Ribs in oven at 400deg F for 25 minutes
  4. Remove ribs and slather BBQ sauce on
  5. Broil ribs at 500deg F for 5 minutes
