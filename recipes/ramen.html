Ramen

Description

Hearthy bowl of ramen that is easy to make!

Ingredients

  • Pork belly
  • Ramen Noodles
  • Broth
  • Corn
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook pork belly into pork chasu using soy sauce
  2. Make broth with pork bones overnight
  3. Add ramen noodles to broth
  4. Add pork and corn to bowl and serve
