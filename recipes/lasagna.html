Lasagna

Description

Super tasty and easy meal

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Cook Ground Beef with oregano
  2. Add first layer of lasagna pasta in tray
  3. Add tomato Sauce and cooked ground beef
  4. Add another layer of lasagna pasta and repeat step 3 and 4 until desired result
  5. Bake for 400degrees F for 45minutes
